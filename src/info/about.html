Adversary Hangman is the classic hangman game with some added bells and whistles, which can be explored by clicking the 'Settings' button.

Dictionary

Any text file where words are separated by whitespace can be used by the program. Words containing non-letters and numbers are ignored. Note that duplicates are not culled. The default word list used is the Brown and LOB Corpus' 5066 most common words. If you want more varied and challenging sets, I recommend looking at Alan Beale's 12dicts Word Lists (http://www.wyrdplay.org/12dicts.html).

Word Choice

Under this category the user can change the word choice algorithm used by the program. Random should be clear enough. Scrabble randomly selects 10 words from the dictionary, and chooses the one yielding the highest base score in Scrabble. Adversary is the most challenging mode. It doesn't choose a word in the beginning at all. Instead, it constructs its word based on your letter choices over the course of the game, aiming to thwart you efforts to win. This mode is inspired by the concept of adversary models, which are used to analyze the performance of computer algorithms.

Word Length and Lives

These should be self-explanatory. Note that depending on the dictionary, there may be word lengths that are invalid, but technically selectable in the slider. If you attempt to choose an invalid length, the program will gently prompt you to select something else. There is also the option, enabled by default, of randomizing the word length after each game.
